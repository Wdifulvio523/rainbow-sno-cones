About Rainbow Sno-Cones

Rainbow Sno-cones was originally opened by two ladies from New Orleans, who ran the shop for five years. My name is Bob Homer, and I bought it from them in 1994. They trained me on how to make the ultimate snoball, New Orleans style. Also they taught me how to mix all the syrups. The original location was on Corrine Drive in Audubon Park, and now I am located in Winter Park on Howell Branch Road and Eastbrook Blvd.

Some people call me Snocone Bob, and I am also known as the Ice Whisperer. In my stroe, you can come in and enjoy a treat. I have over 50 flavors, as well as ice cream flavors that contain evaporated milk.

So come in and enjoy a snocone, snoball, or shaved ice (whatever you want to call it!). I love serving the community and I have customers that now are bringing in their kids and grandkids! We are open year-round, seven days a week. Watch out, you may even get his with a snoball!!
